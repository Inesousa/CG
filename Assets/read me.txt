To play the game:

Go to each scene on Assets -> Scenes and wait for the lighting to bake;
Go to the MainScene on Assets -> Scenes;
Click Play;
See the instructions to know the keys for the abilities;
Choose the level you want to play by walking to its his scene point (water - blue, air - white, earth - brown, red - fire).
